[Groovy](http://groovy-lang.org/) is an agile and dynamic language for
the Java Virtual Machine. It builds upon the strengths of
[Java](Java "wikilink") but has additional power features inspired by
languages like [Python](Python "wikilink"), [Ruby](Ruby "wikilink") and
Smalltalk.
