If you have the "three pane view" of Simple Neurite Tracer enabled,
there is are a couple of key shortcuts that you should know about:

### "Shift" in Three Pane View

If you hold down the  key while moving the mouse in any of the three
panes, the view in the other panes will be synchronized.

### Zooming in Three Pane View

Pressing  or  in any of the three panes will zoom in or out all three of
the panes.

When zoomed in, you can pan around the image by holding down  while
clicking and dragging with the left mouse button in the image window.
