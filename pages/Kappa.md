## Presentation

Kappa is A Fiji plugin for Curvature Analysis.

You can find the documentation in [the Github
repository](https://github.com/brouhardlab/Kappa/tree/master/docs).

## Authors

and is now maintained by [Hadrien Mary](mailto:hadrien.mary@gmail.com).

This work started in 2013 in the [Gary Brouhard
laboratory](http://brouhardlab.mcgill.ca/) at the University of McGill.
