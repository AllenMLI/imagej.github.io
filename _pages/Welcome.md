[[ImageJ]] is an [[open source]] image processing program designed for scientific multidimensional images.

[[ImageJ]] is highly [[Extensibility|extensible]], with thousands of [[plugins]] and [[scripts]] for performing a wide variety of tasks, and a [[Usage|large user community]].</div>
